About us

Solution404 was found by young intelligent people who strive to make a change in our beautiful country Bosnia and Herzegovina. After many projects done by them, they decided to make a company that will continue to make projects, help young people and make contribution to the country. Currently solution404 owns, and together with Centrotrans-Eurolines, maintains a system called “Smart Display”.

OUR SERVICES

Smart Display

"Smart Display" gives you opportunity to promote your advertisements in Centrotrans buses. It gives you 20 seconds for video commercials and 10 seconds for JPG pictures.

Between Dobrinja and Vijecnica operates 6 buses with 104 departures a day. Your advertisement will be displayed over 500 times and shown for more than 4500 passengers in a single day. The advantage of our display compared to all the other displays, is the sound effect of advertisement.

Note: people remember only 20% of what they hear, 30% of what they see and 70% of what they see and hear.

This is the right place for your advertisement!

Technology

Displays are using LED technology that enables better display images than standard LCD displays.

Display size is 22 "(inch) with Full HD resolution (1920x1080) and with support of 16.7 M (million) colors.

One advantage of this display is the angle that is 170 °/160 °, so that all passengers regardless of location can see digital material on the display without problems.
